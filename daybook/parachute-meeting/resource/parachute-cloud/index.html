A book of original entry

The daybook is a cooperative accounting instrument — a ledger of work, time, and value recorded as it happens. What the group makes public accumulates into a shared bloom: the collective record of a day's work.

Built on the Parachute vault protocol. Each entry carries a timestamp, a type, and a scope. The vault handles the rest.
